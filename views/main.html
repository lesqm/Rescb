Dear Colleagues!

On September 20-23, 2016 the XXII Russian Conference with International Participation “X-ray and Electronic Spectra and Chemical Bonding” (RESCB-22) will be orginized in Vladivostok, Russia by Far Eastern Federal University in cooperation with Institute of Chemistry FEB RAS and Nikolaev Institute of Inorganic Chemistry SB RAS.

XXII Russian Conference with International Participation
“X-ray and Electronic Spectra and Chemical Bonding” (RESCB-22)

September 20-23, 2016, Vladivostok, Russia

 The RESCB-22 is the next conference in a series of Russian conferences in the fields of development and application of X-ray and electronic spectroscopy (XPS, AES, NEXAFS, EXAFS, XES) and related methods for study of new chemical compounds and promising materials.The previous two RESCB conferences were held at Novosibirsk Research Center (2010, 2013).

Topics of the Conference:

  • New theoretical methods for electronic structure simulation
  • New scientific equipment
  • Photoelectron spectroscopy and electronic Auger spectroscopy
  • Photoelectrondiffraction
  • X-ray emission spectroscopy. EXAFS,NEXAFS (XANES)
  • Application of XPS for investigation of surface, catalysts, and semiconductors
  • Application of XPS for study ofnanostructured functional materials
